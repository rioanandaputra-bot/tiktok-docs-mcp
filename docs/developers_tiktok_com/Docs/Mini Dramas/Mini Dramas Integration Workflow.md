# Mini Dramas Integration Workflow
This guide explains the comprehensive workflow for building and integrating your mini drama app with TikTok. Outlined are the steps and corresponding guides you must follow to complete the process from start to finish.
## Account registration

| **Action** | **Guide | Section** |
| --- | --- |
| Create an organization that represents your business | [Prepare Your Developer Account | Create an organization](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/perpare-your-developer-account#) |
| Create an app that represents your mini drama app | [Prepare Your Developer Account | Create an app](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/perpare-your-developer-account#) |

## Compliance review

| **Action** | **Guide | Section** |
| --- | --- |
| Verify your organization as a registered business | [Verify Your Business](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/verify-your-business) |
| Verify your organization as a legitimate mini drama apps publisher | [Industry Qualification Review](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/industry-qualification-review) |
| Confirm eligibility of access to protected data (US and EU launch only) | [Launch Your App to U.S. Users](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/launch-your-app-to-us-users-minigames) |

## App setup

| **Action** | **Guide | Section** |
| --- | --- |
| Configure your mini drama app's basic information on the Developer Portal and submit for approval | [[Basic Information Specifications](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/tiktok-minis-basic-information-specifications) Set Up Development Configuration](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/set-up-development-configuration) |
| Add multiple languages to your mini drama app (optional) | [Configure Localization](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/configure-localization) |
| Set up monetization for your mini drama app by enabling features | [[Monetization Overview](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/monetization-overview) Enable Monetization Features](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/enable-monetization-features) |

## Development and debugging

| **Action** | **Guide | Section** |
| --- | --- |
| Develop your mini drama app | [Develop Your Mini drama app | Develop your mini drama app locally](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/develop-your-mini-game#) |
| Integrate your mini drama app with required capabilities | [Develop Your Mini drama app | Integrate APIs for TikTok capabilities](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/develop-your-mini-game#) |

## Publication

| **Action** | **Guide | Section** |
| --- | --- |
| Upload your mini drama app's code package | [Release Your Mini Drama | Upload your code](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/tiktok-minis-release-your-mini-app#) |
| Preview your mini drama app in TikTok | [Release Your Mini Drama | Preview your app in TikTok](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/tiktok-minis-release-your-mini-app#) |
| Submit a version of your mini drama app for review | [Release Your Mini Drama | Submit a version for review](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/tiktok-minis-release-your-mini-app#) |
| Release your mini drama app to users | [Release Your Mini Drama | Release to users](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/tiktok-minis-release-your-mini-app#) |

## Post-publication

| **Action** | **Guide | Section** |
| --- | --- |
| Manage revenue earned from your mini drama app and confirm statements | [Manage Revenue Settlement](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/manage-revenue-settlement) |
| Upload invoices of settled revenue to receive payouts | [Process Invoices and Payouts](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/process-invoices-and-payouts) |

[For more information about how to monetize your mini drama app, refer to the monetization documentation](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/monetization-overview).
[[For detailed API reference guides, refer to TikTok Minis SDK](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/) and TikTok Minis Server APIs](https://developers.tiktok.com/doc/) documentation.
